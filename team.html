Meng Cui - Principal Investigator

Meng Cui is an assistant professor of Electrical Engineering and Biology at Purdue University. He obtained his Ph.D degree from Physics of University of Michigan at Ann Arbor with Jennifer Ogilvie. Between 2010 and 2015, Meng is a Janelia group leader at Howard Hughes Medical Institute. Located in Bindley Bioscience Center, Meng freely pursues his newfound passion of integrated imaging system development. Currently, Meng is learning to build the synergy of teaching, mentoring and research in a fresh scholarly environment. In his spare time, Meng enjoys basketball, jogging, rock climbing, music and cooking.

Bowen Wei received his BS degree in Physics from USTC, China. At Purdue ECE, Bowen is expanding his horizons in innovative approaches of imaging system design and development. In his spare time, Bowen enjoys swimming, table tennis and skateboarding.
